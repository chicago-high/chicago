01 About the Club

A One-of-a-Kind
Private Club

Chicago Highlands is a one-of-a-kind private club in the Chicagoland area featuring a links-style Arthur Hills designed golf course with sweeping views of the Chicago skyline.

The first family-friendly country club to open in the western suburbs in more than 80 years. Our state-of-the-art facilities include a 400-yard grass practice area with three separate practice holes, family-friendly swimming pool with waterslide, clay tennis courts, paddle tennis, full gym, and even a private bowling alley.

Year-Round Activities

Golf, swimming, tennis, bowling, ice skating & more

Masterpiece

Built on 270 acres with elevation changes of over 80 feet, dramatic views of the Chicago skyline, and a style that will make you feel you are playing a modernized links course in Scotland.

The 9th Hole

Named by Golf Digest as one of the 18 best holes built in the U.S. since 2000. A drivable par four perched at the property's highest point with panoramic views of downtown Chicago.
